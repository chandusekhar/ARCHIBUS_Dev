Building  one app for all  mobile platforms
sencha ant -Dapp.name=AppName -Dmobile=all build-app > build.log

Building  one app for  mobile platform of your choice
sencha ant -Dapp.name=AppName  -Dmobile=ios build-app > build.log

Building all apps for all  mobile platforms
sencha ant build-all-mobile-apps > build.log
